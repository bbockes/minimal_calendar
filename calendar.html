Tips
  1. Drag across days on the calendar to select multiple days at once — for Events, this creates an event spanning the range; for Habits, it marks or unmarks all days in the range.
  2. Create multiple habits or calendars to organize different types of tracking.
  3. Export your data from the menu to back up your habits, events, and journal entries. You can import your export later to restore your data.
